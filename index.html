About

This two-year program prepares students with the necessary clinical and administrative knowledge to become entry-level medical assistants.


Students will receive hands-on training and learn how to properly administer injections, take vital signs, record EKGs, implement basic accounting procedures, and understand the fundamentals of patient documentation.


West-MEC programs prepare students to seek one or more industry certification or licensure in their field of study, providing a pathway to economic independence.
